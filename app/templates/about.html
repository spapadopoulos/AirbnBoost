AirbnBoost

Powered by machine learning, AirbnBoost enables users to easily find the Airbnb that suits them best. Features include:

  • Find great deals based on AirbnBoost's pricing algorithm. The algorithm compares similar listings and identifies the ones that are underpriced.
  • Looking for a modern apartment or high aesthetics? Filter listings that match your preferences, without the need to read through their full descriptions.
  • Understand the characteristics of the urban environment surrounding a listing. Is the neighborhood noisy, is the listing close to transportation?

    Currently available in New York City. Coming soon for London, Paris, and Athens.
